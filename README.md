# Flow

**Flow** is an action game created for the **Unity 20th Anniversary Game Jam**.

The project ranked **Top 4 among several hundred submissions**, making it one of the highest-rated projects of the jam.

Armies are rising against you, trying to destroy the Fountain of Youth and steal the Grail.  
To stop them, you must travel through three different timelines and prevent the evil schemes of the **Demons from the past**, the **Legion from the present**, and the **Hunters from the future**.

Use the mysterious power of **Echoes** to create clones of yourself that attack enemies crossing their path.

---

## Game Overview

In **Flow**, the player must defend the Fountain of Youth against waves of enemies coming from three different timelines.

The core gameplay is based on fast movement, attacks, timeline switching, and the use of **Echoes**.  
Echoes allow the player to create temporary clones that reproduce attacks and help control enemy waves.

The player must constantly switch between timelines to manage threats and prevent enemies from overwhelming the map.

---

## Controls

- **Move**: WASD  
- **Look**: Mouse  
- **Dash**: Space, toward the mouse position  
- **Base Attack**: Left Mouse Button  
- **Spin Attack**: Right Mouse Button  
- **Echo**: E + Attack  
- **Switch Timeline**: 1 / 2 / 3 or R
